About ACM NITK

We see a world where computing helps solve tomorrow’s problems – where we use our knnew_owledge and skills to advance the profession and make a positive impact. ACM, the world's largest educational and scientific computing society, delivers resources that advance computing as a science and a profession. ACM provides the computing field's premier Digital Library and serves its members and the computing profession with leading-edge publications, conferences, and career resources. ACM NITK plans and organizes a wide range of events that include technical workshops, guest lectures, online events and various other competitions at NITK, throughout the year.

Kaaryavarta

Events

Apprentice

Apprentice is a B-plan and marketing competition organized by the members of the ACM-NITK chapter. It consists of three rounds: Group Discussions Round, Personal Interview RoundND B - Plan Round.

Shiksha

Shiksha is the computer literacy awareness program conducted by ACM. Under this banner ,volunteers from ACM NITK student chapter teach 8th and 9th standard students of KREC Kannada medium high school the basics of computer science at central computer center every Saturday.

Vidyarthi

Vidyarthi is a social initiative organized by ACM and as a part of this program every Saturday, students from college teach spoken English and reading skills to 10th graders at NITK Kannada medium high school. These students who are about to write the board examination find English as the hardest paper due to lack of practice.

Innovision

A weeklong competition held for freshmen, which tests their analytical,electronics,prototyping,entrepreneurial and literary skills.

Mozilla Workshop

Hands on experience in A-Frame and Mozilla add-ons and promoted open source, in association with Mozilla.

Imagineer-The Makeathon

A 36 hour makeathon to tackle in-house problems, in collaboration with Texas Instruments,Indian Society of Technical Education(ISTE) and Entrepreneurship Cell.

Digital Hackathon

A 24 hour Digital Hackathon in association with the Institution of Engineers(IE).

Knowledge Exchange programs(KEPs)

A knowledge sharing session where specific topics are taught by and to the members.

Linux Installation Drive

A drive to aid freshmen with installing Linux OS in Windows PCs, held in collaboration with the Web Enthusiasts’ Club.

Summer Mentorship Program

Programs conducted over the summer vacations to spread knowledge to the freshmen.
